Our Story

MTS Consulting was founded by Raj Maheshwari, a distinguished member of CPA Australia (CPA), a Fellow of the Institute of Public Accountants (FIPA), a Registered Tax Agent, an SMSF auditor, and a Justice of the Peace (JP).

With over 15 years of experience in public practice, Raj leads the firm, offering business services and tax advice to a diverse clientele, including individuals, small to medium-sized enterprises, and self-managed superannuation funds.

Raj's strong technical and practical expertise is always client-focused, fostering exceptionally strong and loyal relationships with clients.
